About Us

Welcome to CBSEatEase, your one-stop solution for 12th class CBSE board exam preparation! We understand the challenges students face while juggling preparation for various entrance examinations along with board exams. Often, preparation for boards is left for the last two months, leading to stress and anxiety.

At CBSEatEase, we aim to simplify your preparation by providing access to previous year question papers sorted chapterwise, topicwise, and yearwise format with solutions. This not only helps you understand the exam pattern but also enables effective practice. We also provide a unique feature that shows how many times a particular question has been repeated, helping you prioritize your study topics accordingly.

In addition, we offer a 45-day guided study plan that includes a day-wise schedule with links to important topic videos, chapter-wise tests once a week, and tips and techniques to enhance your exam preparation. We introduce you to techniques like the POMODORO technique, which can significantly improve your study efficiency.

Our goal is to make your board exam preparation easier and more effective. Join us at CBSEatEase and embark on a journey towards exam success!
